List of keywords at your disposal


You can use these keywords to navigate through the platform and place orders


  • Open - used to open the platform, normally used like "open Urcella"
  • View - used to view different pages or products, normally used like "view keywords" or "view apples"
  • Select - used to select a product, normally used like "select apples"
  • Remove - used to delete a product from the user's cart, normally used like "remove apples"
  • Quantity - used to set the desired quantity of a specific product, normally used like "quantity 3"
  • Go Back - used to go back to previous page
  • Continue shopping - used to go back to products page from cart page, normally used like "continue shopping"
